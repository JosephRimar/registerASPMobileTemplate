2013 Archery Hunt

The 2013 Aransas Archery Hunt will be held September 28 - October 6. Scout dates are September 21 - 22 and September 27. Bonus Scouting is available September 14 - 15 for those hunters enrolled in the 2013 International Bowhunter Education Program (IBEP) class. For more information about the bonus scouting, click here. There are 400 permits available for this hunt on a first-come, first-serve basis.

2013 Rifle Hunt

The 2013 Aransas Rifle Hunt will be held on the following dates:
  • November 2 - 3
  • November 16 - 17
  • December 7 - 8
  • December 21 - 22
Scout dates will be the day before each hunt. There are 100 permits available for each date on a first-come, first-serve basis.

-- In order to foster the sport of hunting, Aransas NWR offers a reduced rate for youth hunters, age 17 or under. Youth hunters must possess a Texas hunting license, Hunter Education certificate or deferral, and abide by all Refuge regulations. The youth is the sole responsibility of the adult hunter, and must remain within normal voice control of the adult hunter at all times.

A $5.00 processing fee has been added to each archery permit. A $10.00 processing fee has been added to each rifle permit. Permit Fees are refunded (excluding processing fee) if the Refuge cancels your weekend hunt. If only part of the hunt is canceled, refunds will be prorated accordingly.

If you are a youth hunter, you will need to specify your age / birth date during the process and your fees will be discounted to the youth hunt permit amount.
