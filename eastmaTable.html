Hunt Lotteries
Muzzleloader & shotgun white-tailed deer will be issued by lottery. Permits will allow access to Assabet River and Oxbow National Wildlife Refuges for each respective authorized season.
Hunt Permits
There is no lottery for the Archery Deer, Waterfowl, Turkey and Small Game hunt seasons. This application will allow you to receive a permit for any of these hunts.
Hunt Reports
All hunters are required to report their hunt harvest to the Eastern Massachusetts National Wildlife Refuge Complex. Submit your required hunt harvest report now!
Enterance Passes
Entrance Passes
Entrance Passes can be purchased and printed directly online. Passes are required for all individuals 16 years of age or older to access the refuge.
